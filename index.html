How it works

Simple for you, simple for the business. No one needs to touch code or “train” a model manually.

Step 1

Add a business

You plug in the basics: hours, services, pricing, location, contact info, and FAQs. LocalChat AI uses only this data — nothing hallucinated.

Step 2

Send them a snippet

The dashboard generates a one-line script for each business. They paste it into their website, and the chatbot appears in the corner instantly.

Step 3

Chat + leads roll in

Visitors ask questions, get instant answers, and drop their contact info. The owner gets email notifications and a clean lead list in their dashboard.
